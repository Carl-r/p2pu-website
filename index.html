We build learning communities
on the web

Open tools + Community + Design + Support

Playful, networked learning experiences that last

Instead of lonely, anonymous online courses, our communities sustain interest and engagement because folks build them together. We are open and welcoming in everything we do, and help folks generally feel comfy on the internet

Things We Believe in

  • Openess

    Anyone can join, make or contribute to a P2PU experience. We are a 100% open organisation.

  • Power of communty

    The future of learning is not in "disruptive" technologies. It lies in the power of people exploring together.

  • Peers

    Our courses nurture sharing, connection, and help folks feel generally comfortable on the internet.

Things We Know

  • Real people

    We know that working with your peers will keep you engaged in a course.

  • Feedback

    The substantative feedback makes your project better.

  • Comfort

    We know that when you tinker, play and feel comfortable, you'll spend more time on a learning project.

  • Different learning paths

    We know that people learn in different ways, and we offer different paths of engagement.

Who are we?

Meet the community

How do we do stuff?

Community support

Our community of eduthinkers come together in our space, to give each other feedback, develop courses, and recommend tools. Pop over and say hello -- we're a friendly crew.

Social Learning Roundtables

Once a month we come together to discuss important ideas in the future of learning. Conversations have included collaboration with folks from the Khan Academy, DIY.org, Mozilla, EdX and Hack Education.

Reports and documentation

We produce elegant digital reports on all of our lessons learned, so folks can take our blueprint and make it their own.

Talks, conferences, workshops and consulting

We are always eager to talk about our experiences with online learning and how can we help you.
